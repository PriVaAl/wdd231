Site Name

Soften Your Skills

This name reflects the core theme of the project, emphasizing the development and enhancement of essential soft skills for personal and professional growth.

Optional domain availability: softenyourskills.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a platform for users to improve soft skills like communication, leadership, time management, teamwork, and emotional intelligence. It offers interactive tutorials, quizzes, and various learning resources to foster growth and application in real-life situations.

Scenarios

  • 1. How can I improve my leadership and communication skills effectively?
  • 2. Where can I find resources to develop better time management and teamwork abilities?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Deep Blue - Used for headings and accents to promote focus and clarity.

Secondary Color: Forest Green - Used for buttons and hover effects to stimulate creativity and attention.

Accent Color: Red - Used for call-to-action elements to enhance energy and motivation.

Background Color: White - Used for a clean and distraction-free reading experience.

Text Color: Dark Gray - Provides strong contrast for easy readability.
